Map Programming



Using Notepad++, I mapped out my three favorite buildings from my time at the University of Washington. The outlined buildings have pop-ups with a little information. I used html, JavaScript, GeoJSON, and Leaflet to showcase my ability to creat a web map.







ESRI Story Map



I made this story map in ArcGIS Online as a project for a maps and health class at the University of Washington. I used R for computations and data wrangling in order to produce thematic maps. My goal was to show how many people are affected by addiction and mental illness. Moreover, I provided an interactive map of the Seattle's metropolitan area with over 100 different in-patient facilities. The map pop-ups display the name, location, and website for each location.










App creation



Our group made an app for children unable to attend school because of Covid-19. We did surveys of parents with children affected by Covid to see what was needed in an App. This App was fully fuctional but never hosted to the Apple store or the Android market.
